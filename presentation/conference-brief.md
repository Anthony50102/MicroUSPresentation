## The Three Stages

Microelectronics US runs three co-located tracks (stages) simultaneously:

### 1. Semiconductors US

Where compute is fabricated, packaged, and scaled. Topics span fabs, foundries, equipment, materials, advanced packaging, chiplets, yield, reliability, and cost.

### 2. Photonics US

Bandwidth, connectivity, and sensing — silicon photonics, optical interconnects, manufacturing, packaging, and deployment in real systems.

### 3. Embedded Systems US ← Your Stage

Where technology becomes real-world products across automotive, aerospace, industrial, medical, and defense — driven by edge AI, real-time systems, and hardware/software co-design.

## How Your Talk Fits

### Stage Context

Your noon slot on April 22 sits between the morning's safety-critical embedded platforms panel and the afternoon's autonomous driving panel. The audience walking into your talk will already be thinking about:

- How embedded systems need to be smarter, more autonomous
- The gap between what systems can sense and what they can act on
- Real-time decision-making at the edge
- The interplay between hardware, software, and system integration

**This is exactly the setup your talk needs.** You bridge from "systems that sense" to "systems that think and act" — the agentic digital twin.

### Audience Alignment

Your three demo subjects map directly to the stage themes:

| Demo Twin | Stage Theme It Connects To |
|-----------|---------------------------|
| **Edge AI Device** | Embedded AI in Practice — model drift, inference at the edge |
| **Robotic Arm** | Industrial & Edge Systems — predictive maintenance, manufacturing |
| **ADAS Sensor Suite** | Automotive & Mobility — sensor fusion, real-time safety |

### Differentiation

Most talks on this stage will cover specific implementations or vendor solutions. Your talk is different because:

1. **You connect two worlds** — digital twins + agentic AI — that this audience hasn't seen combined
2. **You have a live interactive demo** they can touch on their phones
3. **You speak the embedded systems language** — edge AI, constrained hardware, real-time — not just abstract AI hype
4. **Your demo subjects are their domains** — they'll see their own work reflected in the twin

### Key Opportunity

The Edge AI Foundation co-hosts the networking reception. Edge Impulse has 3 sessions. The afternoon panel covers autonomous driving. Your talk is the connective tissue between these themes — the agentic layer that makes all of them smarter.
